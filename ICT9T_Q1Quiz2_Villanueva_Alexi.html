⋆⁺₊⋆ Howl's Moving Castle ⋆⁺₊⋆

When a shy, timid, and young woman named Sophie is cursed and transformed into an old lady by an evil witch, her only hope to lift the curse depends on a self-centered yet insecure wizard named Howl, along with his companions, whom live in the castle which walks on legs.


˚₊‧ Characters ‧₊˚


Wizard Howl

  • Howl, a 27 year old wizard, is the male protagonist whom lives in a walking house accompanied by Calcifer and Markl. He has a bad reputation despite being a kind person when known better. He is the lover of Sophie and her savior; he is also the ex-lover of the Witch of the West.

Sophie Hatter

  • Sophie Hatter, an 18 year old girl turned 90 by a witch, is the female protagonist who ventures on a journey with Howl and the moving castle to lift her curse. She is a kind, strong-willed, and considerate person who was taken in by Howl to live at his home.

Calcifer

  • Calcifer, a fire demon which lives in Howl's house, is the main power source of the castle and is bound to the hearth. Initially, he seems hostile but later on he warms up to Sophie.

Markl

  • Markl, a 15 year old orphan, is Howl's apprentice and manages Howl's business and affairs when away.

Witch of the Waste

  • The Witch of the Waste, a talented witch with powers that keep her youthful looking, is the main antagonist of the story. She is Howl's ex-lover and the one who cursed Sophie out of jealousy of her youth.

Turnip Head

  • Turnip head, formally known as Prince Justin, was rescued by Sophie in the wasteland. He would later on transform back into his princely state and confess that Sophie was his true love.
